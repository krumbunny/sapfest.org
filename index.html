Thank you for a great 2013 show!

Community, volunteers, vendors, visitors and artists came together for a (mostly) beautiful day. The SAP Library Association appreciates everyone's enthusiasm and good cheer, and we look forward to sponsoring another summer of children's reading program events, as well as taking good care of our branch library.

Whether you are an artist, an art buyer, a local business person, a family on an outing, a volunteer, or just out to enjoy the crowd, you all, we all, make it happen. Thanks for participating, and we hope to see you again in 2014!

Saturday, June 7, 2014

  • Along Como Ave. (Carter Ave to Luther Pl.) St. Paul
  • 10 am to 5 pm

Now in its 45thyear, the Saint Anthony Park Arts Festival brings together artists, musicians, art lovers, families and a supportive community.

This popular event is held near the Saint Paul campus of the University of Minnesota and the Como neighborhood of Minneapolis on the first Saturday in June.

Our festival:

  • Takes place in an attractive neighborhhod setting, and is free
  • Features about 80 artists
  • Benefits the historic St. Anthony Park Branch of the St. Paul Library
  • Engages kids with activities such as the art booth, a weaving project and throwing their own pot.
  • Engages teens and adults with a collaborative art project.
  • Has a used book sale, a plant sale, tasty food and great music
  • Supports summer reading and activity programs, keeping children engaged in reading throughout the summer.
